<ins>Release date?</ins>

Dreadwinter will release in 2026! I'm not sure exactly when yet.

<ins>Platforms?</ins>

Dreadwinter is currently being developed for Steam (Windows/Linux/Steam Deck)<br>
It may be possible to port it to consoles in the future, but it's not in the works for now.

<ins>Multiplayer?</ins>

Maybe, but it would be in a post-release update. It's a lot of work to add in, so I'd prefer to make the
game first.

<ins>Demo?</ins>

Yes, you can try it out on [Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/2803530/Dreadwinter_Demo/)!

<ins>Can I help?</ins>

That depends on what you can do!
I'll be looking for help with localisation closer to release, and maybe someone to help with
music/sound.
Outside of that, I'd like to keep this a solo project.

<ins>More info?</ins>

I post updates on the blog here, and in [Dreadwinter's Steam community hub](https://steamcommunity.com/app/2505230).
I also sometimes post stuff on [Youtube](https://www.youtube.com/@VogyBear), [Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/vogybeargame/) and [Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/vogy-bear.bsky.social).
