<h1><center>Overview</center></h1>
___

<p><center>Dreadwinter is a cozy game about running a fantasy coffee shop. Armed with nothing but a few coffee beans and a dream, take control of Dreadwinter and transform a small, struggling business into a legend!</center></p>
<p>Or don't. There's no correct way to play!</p>

<h1><center>Concept</center></h1>
___

![The Dreadwinter Cafe!](/assets/img/screenshots/cafe-outside-2026.PNG)

<p><center>
Dreadwinter is the name of the cafe, formed of a combination of the names of its owners, Dread and Winter.<br>
Dread is a retired knight, who opened the cafe as a relaxing business venture.<br>
Winter is one of the few remaining Witches, who at one point in time ruled the world.<br>
Due to a combination of their dark pasts, some misunderstandings, and an ineptitude for brewing coffee, Dreadwinter finds itself eternally in the red. With your help, maybe things will turn around!
</p></center>

<h1><center>Features</center></h1>
___

* Play your way - Master your craft or maximise profits, or both!
* Day and night shifts - Serve ordinary humans during the day, and the supernatural at night
* Multiple storylines - The direction of the story will depend on how you play
* Build your community - Get to know your regulars, and they will in turn help you out!
* Become an adventurer - Explore the world and engage in turn-based combat using magical cards
